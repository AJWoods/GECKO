GECKO-Navmesh Tutorial

Navmesh

What is navmesh, and How to place and edit it.

Navmesh is a area made up of linked triangles that dictates where the NPCs can move. Without navmesh, any NPCs will be seen to act somewhat erratically; running in the opposite direction to you, standing in the corner of a room etc. Therefore navmesh is absolutley imperative to any levels with enemies inside, and so you will definitley need some for your tutorial plugin.

Down to business

There are two ways to start with navmesh.The first option is to navigate to the top of the screen, and then go to the drop down menu labelled "Navmesh". Select {"Generate navmesh"} and then wait for a bit whilst it loads. You can also use {something} to only auto navmesh the area you have selected, which can be useful given the incomplete nature of areas of navmesh in certain tiles, such as caves.
